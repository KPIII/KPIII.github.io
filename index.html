THAI MASSAGE

Thai massage aligns the energies of the body using gentle pressure on specific points. It is more like yoga without doing all the work. NECK, BACK AND SHOULDER MASAGE

The focus is on the target areas for people suffering from neck and back massages which are mostly caused by stress and fatigue. With the aid of Moringa oil the therapist deeply massages the specific areas to reduce any inflammation if there is any.

FULL BODY MASAGE

A Full Body massage session will include work on your back, arms, legs, feet, hands, neck and shoulders and for men pectoral muscles in the chest. You generally face down while the therapist starts with your back and shoulders, then moves to the body. Once you flip over, the therapist works their way back up your body, usually ending with the neck and sometimes the scalp.
